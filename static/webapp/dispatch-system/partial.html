Intelligent Auto Dispatch

Cab9 has reinvented dispatch like never before, using algorithms which take into account several user-customisable factors to ensure that the driver dispatched is the most appropriate for both your client and for your business. The dispatch system creates a real-time plot around every booking and assesses live driver queues within it to assign the best driver. This way, your clients never wait too long and the drivers still benefit from queue fairness.

Adaptive To Dispatch Needs

Every driver app is designed according to the respective company’s brand image, and every dashboard of the driver’s account is personalised to show their information. Smart Pricing & Tailored Tariffs

Accurate and flexible pricing is one of the most arduous parts of a private hire business. With Cab9 you simply set and forget, letting the system do all the hard work. Tariffs can be decided on the basis of zones, postcodes, fixed pricing, layered pricing, client specific pricing, vehicle based, and so on. Moreover, the system can be set to round mileage and pricing up or down, add extras for pick-ups in difficult locations, tolls and other journey specifics.

Making the private taxi driving and ride receiving experience easier than ever before for drivers, right at their fingertips.

How it works

  • Tap and go online

    Once the driver logs in with their exclusive account details and mark their availability by tapping ‘Go Online’, they are ready to get bookings and start earning for the day.

  • Accept and bid bookings

    Drivers have the option to accept or reject bookings that have been allotted to them by the dispatcher and grab bookings if they are invited to bid on them in the ‘Job Pool’ section.

  • Complete rides with smart navigation

    Our top-class navigation interface powered by Google Maps guides the driver to take the most ideal routes with the least amount of traffic, distance and wait time.

  • Track your earnings

    Maintaining a record of earnings is simplified on the drivers’ profile, where they can view as well as export all their earnings-related information and pay slips periodically.

  • Rating system

    Drivers’ credibility and performance are elevated through the ratings they receive from passengers after completing every trip.

Easy and Perfect Solution for Drivers

Connecting your bookings with your drivers, CAB9's iPhone and Android app sit front and center within CAB9's strategy. Beside performing all the basic dispatching and booking management functions, apps also allow drivers to have a birds eye or detailed view of their earnings.

Preferential Bidding

When a driver performs exceptionally, they deserve to be rewarded. Top drivers are given priority with an exclusive invitation to bid on bookings from a Job Pool, which means that they get to handpick their bookings before the controllers manually allocate them.

Intuitive Route Management

The driver app compiles the shortest ETA and most hassle-free route between the pickup and drop points with the help of Google Maps-powered navigation to ensure that the driver is always on or before the expected time.

Tailor-made Dashboard

Every driver app is designed according to the respective company’s brand image, and every dashboard of the driver’s account is personalised to show their information. Drivers can keep track of their periodic earnings, bookings completed, performance insights and avail support via the dedicated dashboard.

Real-time Requests and Alerts

The user-friendly interface makes sure that drivers don’t miss out on any bookings. Booking requests and allocations are updated in real-time on the app, be it a customer looking for a ride from the airport or a dispatcher setting up transport for a meeting, and the driver can respond at once by accepting or rejecting them.

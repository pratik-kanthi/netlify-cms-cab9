Reliable Private Hire Round-the-clock

Offering customers a simple and sleek outlook into the private taxi booking process, all the way from the booking initiation to completing trip fare payment

How it works

  • Create an account

    Customers just need an email id and contact number to create an account with their chosen private hire service, this can be done either through the website of the taxi company or their dedicated mobile app.

  • Book a ride

    The booking page will enable the customer to select when they need the taxi, their destination, preferred vehicle type and payment method.

  • Track your taxi

    After being matched to the nearest available driver, the customer can check the ETA of the driver from the ‘Now’ tab and know when their taxi is approaching their pick-up location in real-time.

  • Settle the fare

    Depending on the payment mode selected at the time of booking, the passenger can pay for the trip by cash, pre-added card, or on account. .

  • Rate your ride

    Rating the driver out of 5 stars is a great way for passengers to share feedback on the positive experience they have had with the driver and the company.

On-demand Booking

Book rides for occasions that have been planned in advance or at any time on-the-go. The passenger app especially comes in handy when passengers need to get somewhere from the airport. The Cab9 software tracks the arrival of the flight linked to the booking, so drivers will be ready and on time for the pickup, guaranteed.

Flexible Vehicle Options

Depending on the passenger’s vehicle needs and the number of passengers, there are various vehicle types available to choose from. The vehicle types offered range from Passenger and Black Taxi to Executive and MPV. The former are typically the go-to option for a cost-effective and comfortable ride, while the latter are ideal as premium transportation for corporate purposes.

Instantaneous Trip Tracking

Every booking route is automatically planned through the integrated maps system to take the shortest and most efficient way. The passenger will be notified at every step of the booking, including vehicle details, ETA of the driver, and other trip updates. Once the booking begins, the passenger will be able to see the trip route and the arrival time at their destination. Apart from tracking current bookings via the ‘Now’ tab, passengers will have a record of their future and past bookings, in the ‘Upcoming’ and ‘History’ tabs respectively.

Multiple Payment Methods

Cab9 system supports three different fare payment options i.e. cash, credit card and On Account. The passenger app is integrated with Stripe and Judopay to facilitate quick and no-hassle payment completion. The upfront pricing model shows a passenger their fare even before they make the booking, so they are aware of exactly how much they would have to pay and can choose their payment method accordingly. In case of business bookings, the payment can be pre-authorised and invoiced directly to the concerned corporate account.
